Troubleshooting Terminal

Payments may only be processed when the terminal displays the icon shown on the top-right corner of the screen. Do not attempt a sale.

= Ready to Process Payments
⚡ Terminal not showing cloud icon ? Complete the following steps in order until it appears.
1
Allow 2 minutes to elapse

Following any restart, the terminal requires time to fully establish its connection.
Do not intervene — wait the entire two minutes before taking further action.

Icon still not showing? → Proceed to Step 2
2
Force Stop the Delta1st application

A standard close is insufficient. The application must be fully terminated through the device settings.

How to Force Stop Delta1st:
  1. Press the Home button to return to the main screen.
  2. Open Settings, then select Apps.
  3. Locate Delta1st and select Force Stop, then confirm.
  4. Return to the home screen, reopen Delta1st, and wait 2 minutes.
Icon still not showing? → Proceed to Step 3
3
Restart the terminal
  1. Press and hold the Power button until the menu appears.
  2. Select "Restart." Do not select "Power Off."
  3. Allow the terminal to complete startup — approximately 3 minutes.
Icon still not showing? → Proceed to Step 4
4
Verify the network connection

Begin by testing internet access on your phone. If your phone has no internet, the terminal cannot connect either.

Wi-Fi: Confirm the terminal is on the correct network. If the network name or password has changed, contact your network provider.

Ethernet Cable: Ensure the cable is firmly connected at both ends. Test the port with another device to confirm it has internet access. If it does not, contact your network provider.

Icon still not showing? → Proceed to Step 5
5

Do not attempt to process any sales with the terminal in this state. If a secondary terminal is available, use it while the issue is being resolved.

❌ DO NOT -
🔁Restart the terminal multiple times in rapid succession. Each restart resets the connection process and delays recovery.
💳Attempt to complete a sale when the icon is not displayed in the top-right corner of the terminal.
⏻Power off the terminal entirely. Always select "Restart" from the power menu.
✅ Best Practices
⏱️Wait a minimum of three minutes between each restart attempt before concluding the terminal has not recovered.
👁️Confirm the icon is visible in the top-right corner of the terminal before initiating any sale.
📱Ensure there is only one payment application installed on the terminal at all times.
📞Contact technical support promptly if the issue remains unresolved after completing all four steps above.

Terminal Icon Reference Guide

Use this page to identify your terminal's connection status at a glance. Post this guide at the register for easy access by all staff members.

Icon Status What It Means & What To Do
Cloud + Plug
 ✅ Ready to Sell The terminal is fully connected and operational. It is able to receive payment amounts, process gratuities, and complete transactions. You may proceed with sales normally.
Plug Only
 ❌ Do Not Sell Partial connection — the terminal cannot receive instructions from the point-of-sale system. The terminal has limited internet access but is unable to communicate with the register. Follow the steps on Page 1. Contact support if the issue persists.
Cloud Only
 ❌ Do Not Sell Partial connection — the terminal cannot process payments correctly. The terminal is receiving data but is unable to complete transactions. Follow the steps on Page 1. Contact support if the issue persists.
No Signal
 🚫 Stop — No Connection The terminal has lost all connectivity. No sales may be processed under any circumstances. Begin immediately from Step 1 on Page 1. Contact technical support without delay if the issue is not resolved within 10 minutes.

Important: Allow 1–5 minutes after each restart before processing any sale. It is entirely normal to observe intermediate icons — such as "Plug Only" or "Cloud Only" — during the startup sequence. This does not indicate a problem. Do not restart the terminal again during this period, as doing so will reset the connection process and further delay readiness. Wait until the terminal displays the full cloud and plug icon in the top-right corner before proceeding with any transaction.
