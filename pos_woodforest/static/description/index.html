Everything you need to know.

What do I need to get started?
You'll need an active Woodforest merchant account and Odoo Point of Sale. Install the integration, connect your credentials, and you're ready to accept payments.
How long does setup take?
Most merchants are live in minutes. No middleware. No complex configuration. Just connect and go.
What payment types are supported?
Chip. Tap. Contactless. All processed directly inside Odoo through your Woodforest terminal.
Can I send secure payment links?
Yes. Generate secure payment links from Odoo and get paid remotely in seconds.
How are refunds processed?
Refunds are initiated inside Odoo and synced automatically to Woodforest. Fully tracked. Fully reconciled.
Is this an official Woodforest integration?
Yes. This is the official Woodforest Acceptance integration for Odoo, built for reliability and long-term support.
Why choose Woodforest for Odoo?
Bank-backed processing. Intelligent routing for higher approvals. Real merchant support when it matters.
